Requirements:
- theme: merge + hex
- viewport: 720x720
- code + assets (.wasm + .data): 64mb
- library: raylib
- langauge: c99

You, a wizard, are in the fast (left) lane on a freeway. People in the lane to the right 
are trying to merge into your lane, and you need to cast hexes to destroy them.

The perspective is first person: you in the driver seat. You can see out the left
part of your windsheild. You have a spedometer and a rear view mirror as well. 

You cast hexes by a draging to draw one of a preset shape like a star or a spiral.

---

game starts by you waking up (you were sleeping while driving)
HUD is google maps phone or something
